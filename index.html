Research Areas

What I Study

🤖

AI in Accounting

Exploring how large language models and machine learning can be applied to accounting research, including automated financial analysis and intelligent disclosure processing.

📄

Corporate Disclosure Analysis

Computational text analysis of annual reports and corporate filings to understand managerial communication, information asymmetry, and disclosure quality.

📊

Empirical Accounting Research

Applying econometric methods — panel data, difference-in-differences, fixed effects — to financial reporting and corporate governance questions.

🔍

Natural Language Processing

Using NLP techniques including sentiment analysis, readability scoring, and topic modelling to extract insights from large corpora of financial text data.
